Casserole

Description

Casserole is a delicious dish that is enjoyed in many homes across America. It can be made with many ingredients and leftovers from around the house and is an easy "one pot" recipe. This version of this dish uses only shelf stable or frozen ingredients to help with the ease of cooking as a last minute meal.

Ingredients

  • Medium Shell Pasta - 1 box
  • Cream of Chicken Soup - 1 can
  • Mayonaisse - 3 heaping tablespoons
  • Curry Powder - 1 teaspoon (or to taste)
  • Lemon juice - 1 squeeze
  • Frozen peas - 1/3 bag (or to taste)
  • Solid White Albacore Tuna - 2 cans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions to an al dente firmness.
  3. Mix Cream of Chicken Soup, Mayonaisse, Curry Powder, and Lemon Juice in a casserole dish.
  4. Stir in Frozen Peas, Solid White Albacore Tuna, and cooked pasta.
  5. Cook 40 min in preheated oven.
