Grilled Cheese

Description

A very simple dish with easy to source ingredients, grilled cheese sandwiches delight many the world over. This is a simplified and perfected version of this classic recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread - 2 slices
  • Mayonaisse - approx 1/2 tablespoon
  • American Cheese - 2 slices

Steps

  1. Spread Mayonaisse on one side of Sourdough Bread until lightly coated.
  2. Place one slice of bread, mayonaisse side down in a hot frying pan.
  3. Place cheese on top of bread and the other bread on top mayonaisse side up.
  4. Fry until golden brown on each side, then cut diagonally and enjoy.
