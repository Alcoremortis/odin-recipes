Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an easy to make noodle dish that is prized for its deliciousness and association to a certain orange cartoon cat.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage - 1 pound
  • Lean Ground Beef - 3/4 pound
  • Minced Onion - 1/2 cup
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, crushed
  • Crushed tomatoes - 1 can
  • Tomato Sauce - 2 cans
  • Tomato Paste - 2 cans
  • Water - 1/2 cup
  • White Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley - 4 tbsp
  • Dried Basil Leaves - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Italian Seasoning - 1/2 tsp
  • Fennel Seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Ground Black Pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Lasagna noodles - 12
  • Ricotta Cheese - 16 oz
  • Egg - 1
  • Sliced Mozzarella Cheese - 3/4 pound
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese - 3/4

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water.
  4. Season with sugar, 2 tbps of parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon of salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper
  5. Simmer, covered for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna in boiling water for 8-10 min. Drain noodles, rinse with cold water.
  7. Combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tbsp parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  8. Preheat oven to 375F.
  9. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of baking dish, arrange 6 noodles over meat sauce, spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese. Top with 1/3 of mozzarrella cheese slices. Repeat.
  10. Cover in foil and bake for 25 min. Remove foil and bake for another 25 min.
